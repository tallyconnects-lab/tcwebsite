### How Auto-Mapping Works

TallyConnects doesn't need a fixed template. Bring your own Excel file — in any format — and the smart 3-level auto-mapping engine handles the rest.

Level 1: Exact Match
Matches column headers exactly (e.g., "Invoice No" matches "Invoice No").

Level 2: Synonym Match
Recognizes common variations (e.g., "Bill Number", "Inv No", "Invoice Number" all match to Invoice No).

Level 3: Fuzzy Match
Uses word-token matching for partial matches (e.g., "Party/Customer Name" matches to "Party Name").

No rigid templates. No reformatting your data. Just import and go.
